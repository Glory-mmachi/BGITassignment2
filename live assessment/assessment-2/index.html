Vanilla Cake Recipe

Vanilla cakes Recipe

List of ingredients

  • 150g of Butter
  • 150g of Sugar
  • 3 large Eggs
  • 1tsp Vanilla flavor
  • 250g of Flour
  • 250g of Milk

Preparation

  • Whip the butter and sugar for about 5mins
  • Add eggs on at a time and mix
  • Add flavor
  • Add flour and sugar alternatively

Cooking time: 45mins

Difficulty: Moderate
